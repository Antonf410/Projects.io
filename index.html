About Me:

I'm Anton Farhoud, a data analyst. I dive deep into data, uncovering insights and trends. I direct my creativity into music, blending the precision of music with the artistry of sound. Whether I'm analyzing complex datasets or crafting melodies, I strive to bring harmony and clarity to every project.

  • More:

I was born on October 4, 1994, in Shefa-'Amr, Israel. In 2002, at the age of 8, I began learning music. In 2003, I was encouraged to play in the church based on my basic music knowledge, which pushed me to learn music independently and grow from my mistakes. Over the years, I improved significantly, eventually becoming a worship leader, band instructor, and a conference coordinator.

In 2019, I graduated with honors with a Bachelor's degree in Music from Zefat Academic College. In 2021, I completed a multi-age teaching certificate with honors from Levinsky College of Education in Tel Aviv. I worked for three years in elementary schools, analyzing musical pieces and songs, extracting key elements, and teaching them to my students.

In 2024, I completed a Data Analyst course at John Bryce College in Haifa, where I gained comprehensive tools for working as an analyst across various fields, including Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI.

I continue to learn from every experience, whether through self-study, developing new ideas, or taking online courses, all of which contribute to my growing expertise in analysis.
